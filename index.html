Bio

Jana Cvetinovska is a PhD student at the University of St. Gallen specializing in stochastic optimization, decision theory, and control under uncertainty. Her research lies in distributionally robust optimization, convex duality, and measure-theoretic probability, with applications to dynamic programming and Markov decision processes. She previously obtained a Master’s degree in Quantitative Economics and Finance at the University of St. Gallen and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at Bocconi University.
